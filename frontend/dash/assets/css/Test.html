The Mission

Given the increasingly complex nature of the problems we face, we believe that the way forwards is through fostering informed dialogue between all of those involved in the solution. This includes - but is by no mean limited to - researchers, technologists, industry leaders, policy writers and the general public.

Our approach towards reaching a consensus on what entails the safe design, development and deployment of technology here in Australia involves adopting the different lenses of those involved in order to increase our situational awareness and make more informed decisions.

Our mission is to establish a centralised platform for honest, open discussion about what our ethical and societal values are in relation to the use of AI in Australia. In doing so, this will enable us to collaboratively co-design a technological future to align with those values.

Who We Are

We are a nationwide Australian non-profit think tank dedicated to addressing AI safety.

Our focus is on the shared pursuit of knowledge and responsible action. We believe in the power of collective insights and diverse perspectives to help navigate the increasingly complex landscape of AI safety.

Through collaborative education and sense making, we can shape a future where AI technologies are developed and deployed responsibly, aligning with the societal values and safety considerations that we choose.
